History of Chess

Chess originated in India around the 6th century AD, evolving from Chaturanga. It spread to Persia and then Europe, becoming the game we know today.

Basic Rules

The game is played on an 8x8 board with each player having 16 pieces.
  • Objective: Checkmate the opponent's King.
  • Each piece has its own movement rules.

Famous Chess Champions

Magnus Carlsen

Reigning World Champion since 2013, known for his strategic genius.

Garry Kasparov

World Champion from 1985 to 2000, famous for deep preparation.

Popular Strategies

  1. Control the center to maximize piece mobility.
  2. Develop pieces early for better positioning.
  3. Ensure King safety, often through castling.
